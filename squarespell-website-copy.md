### Section 9: FAQ

**Headline:**
Questions? We've got answers.

**Q: How does Squarespell work?**
A: Paste your Squarespace URL into Squarespell. Our AI reads your site, detects your branding, and generates a complete quiz funnel — questions, results, and lead capture — in about 30 seconds. You then copy a single line of embed code into your Squarespace site and you're live.

**Q: Do I need any coding skills?**
A: None at all. Squarespell generates everything for you. Embedding is as simple as pasting one script tag into a Squarespace Code Block — we show you exactly where.

**Q: Will the quiz match my website's design?**
A: Yes. Our AI automatically detects your brand colors, fonts, and style from your Squarespace site. The quiz looks like a native part of your website.

**Q: What happens with the leads I capture?**
A: Every lead (name, email, quiz result) is stored in your Squarespell dashboard. You get instant email notifications, can export to CSV, or connect to tools like Zapier to push leads to your email platform.

**Q: Can I customize the quiz after it's generated?**
A: Absolutely. You can edit questions, change result descriptions, update CTAs, and adjust settings from your dashboard. The AI gives you a great starting point, and you fine-tune it.

**Q: Does it work on mobile?**
A: Yes. The quiz embed is fully responsive and looks great on phones, tablets, and desktops. It automatically adjusts to your visitor's screen size.

**Q: What if I don't have a Squarespace site?**
A: Squarespell is built specifically for Squarespace, but the embed code works on any website that supports custom code blocks — WordPress, Wix, Shopify, or even a plain HTML page.

**Q: Can I try it before signing up?**
A: Yes! Paste your URL right here on this page and see a preview of your quiz — no account needed. If you like what you see, create a free account to publish it.
