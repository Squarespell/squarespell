## 1.1 — FEATURES THE CURRENT PAGE MISSES ENTIRELY

### 🔴 CRITICAL GAPS (conversion-killing omissions)

**1. VIDEO QUESTIONS**
The quiz editor shows a full embedded video inside Q1. Users can add a video to any question — not just images. This is a massive creative differentiator: Squarespace photographers, coaches, and course creators can put their brand video *inside* the quiz question itself. No competitor does this at this price point. The V3 page never mentions it.
→ **SEO keyword unlocked:** "interactive video quiz builder", "video quiz for Squarespace"

**2. REVENUE ATTRIBUTION ($68,420 tracked in dashboard)**
The dashboard shows a "Revenue attributed" metric — $68,420 tracked in one period. This is not a testimonial number or an estimate. It is a live tracked dashboard metric. Every quiz completion that leads to a purchase is attributed. The V3 page buries revenue impact in testimonials. It should lead with it.
→ **SEO keyword unlocked:** "quiz revenue tracking", "quiz conversion attribution"

**3. QUIZ SHARING / VIRALITY (4,982 shares tracked)**
The dashboard tracks "Shares generated" as a first-class metric (+38.9% growth shown). Quiz results are shareable — users share their personal outcome on social media. This means every completed quiz can generate free impressions. This is a free distribution flywheel. V3 never mentions it.
→ **Messaging angle:** "Quizzes your visitors share — turning leads into reach."

**4. HIGH INTENT AUTO-CLASSIFICATION**
The Leads dashboard auto-tags every lead as "High intent", "New", or "Low score" based on their quiz score. A salesperson opens the dashboard and immediately knows who to call first — without manual review. V3 describes lead scoring but never explains the automatic intent classification as a sales prioritization feature.
→ **Messaging angle:** "Your hottest leads, already sorted. No manual review."

**5. SCORE-TRIGGERED AUTOMATIONS**
The Automations page description reads: *"Trigger actions automatically when leads complete quizzes, reach scores, or get tagged."* This means automations fire on score threshold (e.g., "if score > 75, send the premium offer email"). This is more sophisticated than simple completion-triggered flows. V3 mentions automations but only describes completion triggers.
→ **SEO keyword unlocked:** "score-based email automation", "lead score triggered workflow"

**6. VISUAL AUTOMATION FLOW BUILDER**
The Automations UI shows a node-based visual builder (email → trigger → people → tag) — similar to Zapier or ActiveCampaign. This is not just "set up an email sequence." It's a no-code automation canvas. V3 calls it "email sequences" which undersells it dramatically.
→ **Messaging angle:** "A visual automation builder — no Zapier account required."

**7. NATIVE EMAIL PLATFORM (25,000 emails/month included)**
Email Campaigns page shows "Monthly Email Usage: 0 / 25,000 emails." Squarespell Quiz has a built-in email platform — not just integrations. You can run campaigns, automations, and quiz result emails without connecting Mailchimp or Klaviyo. V3 only frames email as an integration story, not a built-in capability.
→ **Messaging angle:** "Send up to 25,000 emails a month — included. No Mailchimp required."

**8. PRODUCT-TO-OUTCOME MAPPING (Commerce Integration)**
The Commerce page says: *"Connect your Squarespace store and map products to quiz outcomes."* This means individual Squarespace store products are linked to specific quiz outcome paths. A visitor who gets "Outcome: Dry Skin" sees the exact Hydration Serum SKU from the store. V3 mentions "product recommendation quiz" generically but never explains the technical mechanism — map outcomes to real products.
→ **Messaging angle:** "Map quiz outcomes to your actual store products — one click."
→ **SEO keyword unlocked:** "Squarespace product recommendation quiz", "quiz for Squarespace Commerce"

**9. TIMED QUESTIONS (02:45 timer visible)**
The quiz editor shows a timer (02:45) in the top right. Individual questions can have a time limit. This enables gamified quizzes, timed assessments, knowledge tests. Not mentioned anywhere in V3.
→ **Messaging angle:** "Add timers to questions — gamify, assess, urgency-drive."

**10. QUIZ RATING METRIC (9.2/10 avg tracked)**
Dashboard shows "Avg. quiz rating: 9.2 / 10 (+0.4)." The platform tracks how quiz takers rate the quiz experience itself. This feedback loop is a quality optimization signal. Not mentioned in V3.

**11. QUESTION-LEVEL LAYOUT OPTIONS**
The editor toolbar shows: "+ Option | Timer | Branch | Layout" — Layout is a per-question setting. Users can change the visual layout of each question individually. Not explained.

Settings → Completion → Redirect URL + Redirect delay (seconds): 5. After quiz completion, users can redirect visitors to any URL (a product page, a booking page, a checkout page) with a configurable delay. Not mentioned. This is a conversion action, not just a UX feature.

Settings → Transition: Slide. Question-to-question animation. Polished UX signal. Small but worth mentioning in the "feels native" story.

**20. 16 TEMPLATES ACROSS 13+ INDUSTRY CATEGORIES**

## 1.4 — RECOMMENDED PAGE FLOW & CONVERSION LOGIC

**Why this order:**

```
HERO (URL input hook — do the thing now)
    ↓
PROOF BAR (validate with numbers)
    ↓
THE MAGIC MOMENT (AI scans your URL — show it visually)
    ↓
HOW IT WORKS — 3 steps (fastest path to clarity)
    ↓
QUIZ BUILDER FEATURE (depth + differentiators: video, branching, timer, A/B)
    ↓
LEAD CAPTURE + SCORING (email gate → auto-scored → intent-classified)
    ↓
AUTOMATION (score-triggered flows, visual builder, 25k emails native)
    ↓
ANALYTICS + REVENUE (the dashboard — $68k attribution, completion time, quiz rating)
    ↓
COMMERCE (product mapping — the e-commerce killer feature)
    ↓
TEMPLATES (16 templates, 13 industries — fastest path to live)
    ↓
CONVERSION DATA (40.1% vs 1.2% — the case to switch)
    ↓
COMPARISON (vs Typeform — 5 rows, clean)
    ↓
TESTIMONIALS (3 cards, real outcomes)
    ↓
PRICING (toggle, 3 plans, ROI calculator)
    ↓
FAQ (10 questions max)
    ↓
FINAL CTA
```

**Why this works:**
- Hero captures interest + plants the URL-input hook (the wow moment)
- Proof bar validates before they read a word
- "Magic moment" section (AI scan) is the primary differentiator — show it early
- How It Works gives structure before features
- Features flow from creation → capture → automation → analysis — the natural product workflow
- Commerce before templates because product-mapped quizzes are the highest-value use case
- Conversion data comes after features (credibility established first)
- Comparison is short — modern SaaS users expect it; don't hide it
- Pricing before FAQ so price objections are addressed before detailed Q&A
- FAQ is 10 questions — not 35

**Q1: Can you add a quiz to Squarespace?**
Yes. Squarespace has no native quiz feature. Squarespell Quiz adds a fully branded, natively-embedded quiz to any Squarespace page in one click — on every plan, including Personal. No Code Injection, no developer required.

**Q2: Does Squarespace have a built-in quiz builder?**
No. As of 2026, Squarespace does not offer a quiz builder. Third-party tools are required. Squarespell Quiz is the only quiz tool built specifically for Squarespace — every other option requires manual embed codes and doesn't import your brand automatically.

**Q3: How long does setup take?**
The median time from account creation to first published quiz is 11 minutes — including sign-up, AI generation, edits, and embedding on your Squarespace page.

**Q4: Do I need to know how to code?**
No. The AI generates the quiz. The builder is drag-and-drop. The embed is one click. Custom CSS is available on Pro for those who want it — entirely optional.

**Q5: Will the quiz match my Squarespace site's design?**
Yes. When you connect your URL, Squarespell Quiz reads your site's CSS — your font stack, color palette, and logo — and applies it to every element of the quiz automatically. The quiz looks like it was built with your site, not dropped onto it.

**Q6: What types of quizzes can I build?**
Product recommendation quizzes, lead generation quizzes, personality quizzes, assessment quizzes, style quizzes, quiz funnels, and timed knowledge tests. All 16 templates are industry-specific and launch-ready.

**Q7: Does it work with Squarespace Commerce / my online store?**
Yes. On the Business plan, you can connect your Squarespace store and map specific products to quiz outcomes. A visitor who gets a personalized result sees the exact products from your catalog — not generic recommendations.

**Q8: Does it slow down my site?**
No. Squarespell Quiz uses native code — not a third-party iframe. The quiz adds approximately 8–12KB to the page. PageSpeed scores on pages with Squarespell quizzes are within 2–3 points of the same page without the quiz.

**Q9: What happens to my data if I cancel?**
Nothing is deleted on cancellation. All lead data, quiz configurations, and analytics are preserved. You can export everything at any time. Reactivate and everything is exactly as you left it.

**Q10: Can I use Squarespell Quiz for multiple Squarespace sites?**
Yes. One account connects to multiple Squarespace URLs. Business plan includes team seats for multi-person management. Shared quizzes are visible across the team workspace.
