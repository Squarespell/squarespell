Honoring Mrs. Thompson
 

After 12 years of inspiring young minds, Mrs. Linda Thompson is retiring from South Elementary.
 
Beloved for her passion for literature and her dedication to every student, she has left a lasting mark on our classrooms, our community, and our hearts. From leading clubs to cheering on students at events, Mrs. Thompson has given her all — and we are so grateful.
 
Thank you, Mrs. Thompson.
 
You’ll always be part of the South Elementary family.
