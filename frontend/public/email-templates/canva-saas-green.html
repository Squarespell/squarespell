Hack of the month:
 
Time management
 
Stay on top of your schedule with three quick tips:
 
•3-Task Rule: Focus on your top three priorities each day.
•Time Blocking: Set chunks of time for deep work, email, or breaks.
•Two-Minute Rule: If it takes less than two minutes, do it right away.
 
Small changes, big results — less stress and more control over your day.
