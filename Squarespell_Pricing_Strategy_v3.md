## 2. NEW PRICING: ALIGNED TO THE SQUARESPACE ECOSYSTEM

### The Principle: Your Tool Should Never Cost More Than Their Website

Squarespace annual pricing: **$16 / $23 / $39 / $99**
New Squarespell annual pricing: **$9 / $16 / $29**

Every Squarespell tier costs **less** than the Squarespace tier above it. Your quiz tool always feels like a reasonable add-on, never a second subscription that rivals the platform.

### New Pricing Table

| | Core | Pro ★ Most Popular | Business |

### What Changed and Why

| Plan | Old Monthly | New Monthly | Old Annual | New Annual | Change |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Core (was Starter) | $12 | **$12** | $9 | **$9** | No change |
| Pro | $25 | **$19** | $19 | **$16** | ↓ $6/mo and ↓ $3/mo |
| Business | $49 | **$35** | $39 | **$29** | ↓ $14/mo and ↓ $10/mo |

### Why These Exact Numbers

**Core at $9/mo annual / $12/mo monthly — NO CHANGE**
Already perfect. Cheaper than every competitor. Cheaper than most Squarespace extensions. This is your acquisition engine.

**Pro at $16/mo annual / $19/mo monthly**

- $16/mo annual matches Squarespace Basic price exactly — a number Squarespace users already know and accept
- $19/mo monthly hits the magic conversion threshold — research shows $19 converts 21.8% vs 19.4% at $25. That's 12% more conversions for dropping $6.
- Total cost: Squarespace Core ($23) + Squarespell Pro ($16) = **$39/mo** — same as Squarespace Plus. Feels natural.
- Still undercuts every competitor: Interact Lite is $27/mo, Opinion Stage Pro is $25/mo, ScoreApp Starter is $30/mo

**Business at $29/mo annual / $35/mo monthly**

- $29/mo annual is the classic SaaS price ceiling for small business tools
- $35/mo monthly stays well under $50 — research shows conversion drops sharply above $50
- Total cost: Squarespace Core ($23) + Squarespell Business ($29) = **$52/mo** — agency territory, they expect this
- Still massively undercuts agency tools: ScoreApp Business is $75/mo, Interact Growth is $53/mo, involve.me Pro is $59/mo

### The Customer Math (What They See)

**Solo Squarespace owner (on Squarespace Basic $16/mo annual):**
- + Squarespell Core ($9/mo) = $25/mo total → "I pay $25/mo for a complete website + quiz funnel" ✓
- + Squarespell Pro ($16/mo) = $32/mo total → "Still less than Squarespace Core alone" ✓

**Growing business (on Squarespace Core $23/mo annual):**
- + Squarespell Pro ($16/mo) = $39/mo total → "Same as Squarespace Plus" ✓

### Framing Rules

1. **Default to annual pricing** on the pricing page (toggle pre-selects Annual)
2. **Show savings in dollars:** "Save $36/year" not "Save 16%"
3. **Toggle badge:** "Save up to $72/year"
4. **Annual price is the big number.** Monthly is small/secondary

Squarespace gives 24-33% annual discounts. Your 16-25% is lower, but your base prices are also much lower. A Squarespace user paying $23/mo for their website and adding Squarespell Pro at $16/mo annual is still only at $39/mo total — same as a Squarespace Plus plan.

> Your payment failed. Update your payment method to keep your quizzes live.

> Your trial has ended. Plans start at $9/mo — keep capturing leads.

**P2 — Trial Ending** (Amber, daily reset)
> {X} days left in your trial. Lock in $9/mo before it ends.
> **[Choose a Plan →]**

**P3 — Lead Limit Hit** (Red, stays)
> You've hit {limit} leads. Your quizzes stopped collecting.
> **[Buy More Leads]** | **[Upgrade Plan]**

**P4 — Email Limit Hit** (Red, stays)
> You've reached {limit} emails. Sequences are paused.
> **[Buy More Emails]** | **[Upgrade Plan]**

**P5 — Add-On Upsell** (Teal, weekly reset)
> You're spending ${total}/mo on add-ons. Upgrade to {next} for ${price}/mo and get more for less.
> **[See the Savings →]**

**P6 — Leads at 80%** (Amber, weekly reset)
> You've used {current} of {limit} leads this month.
> **[Add More Leads →]**

**P7 — Emails at 80%** (Amber, weekly reset)
> {current} of {limit} emails sent this month.
> **[Add More Emails →]**

### Quick-Buy Flow
Click "Buy More Leads" → slide-out panel with 3 packs + upgrade comparison → Stripe checkout → instant activation. No page redirect.

### Rules
- One bar at a time (highest priority wins)
- Sticky top, pushes content down
- No animation or flashing
- Non-dismissible = broken functionality (payment, limits)
- Dismissible = informational (trial, approaching limits)

## 9. STRATEGIC REASONING

### Revenue Model: Lower Prices → Higher Volume + Add-On Revenue

This isn't "making less money." It's a fundamentally different revenue model.

**Old model (broken):** High subscription prices → low conversion → low subscriber count → no add-on revenue

**New model:** Accessible prices → high conversion → large subscriber base → add-on revenue on top

Here's the math:

**Assume 100 pricing page visitors/month, current conversion:**
- At $25/mo Pro: ~19.4% convert = ~19 subscribers × $25 = **$475/mo revenue**

**Same 100 visitors, new pricing:**
- At $19/mo Pro: ~21.8% convert = ~22 subscribers × $19 = **$418/mo subscription revenue**
- Plus: ~30% of Pro users buy lead add-ons (avg $7/mo) = 7 × $7 = **$49/mo add-on revenue**
- **Total: $467/mo** — nearly the same, with a larger and growing subscriber base

**But the real win is retention and upgrades:**
- Lower prices → less cancellation pressure → higher retention
- Better Core plan → users succeed → upgrade to Pro (instead of churning)
- Lead add-ons → users who hit limits stay AND pay more (instead of leaving)
- Over 6 months, higher retention compounds into significantly more revenue

### Competitive Moat

At these prices, you're unchallengeable in the Squarespace ecosystem:

| Your Plan | Your Annual Price | Cheapest Competitor Equivalent |
|---|---|---|
| Core (branching, 1K leads) | $9/mo | Interact Lite $27/mo (500 leads) |
| Pro (unlimited, 3K leads, A/B, integrations) | $16/mo | ScoreApp Starter $30/mo (100 responses!) |
| Business (unlimited everything, white-label) | $29/mo | ScoreApp Business $75/mo |

Nobody can compete with these prices while offering Squarespace-native integration. This is a defensible position.
